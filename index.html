Getting Started

  1. Grant Photo Access: When you first open SwipeSort, grant access to your photo library
  2. Create Albums: Go to the Albums tab and create custom albums for organizing your photos
  3. Start Swiping: Return to the Photos tab and start swiping through your photos
  4. Organize or Delete: Swipe right to add photos to albums, swipe left to delete, or swipe up to skip
  5. Undo Mistakes: Use the undo button to reverse any action

Frequently Asked Questions

📱 Does SwipeSort work offline?

Yes! All photo processing happens on your device. No internet connection is required, and your photos never leave your device.

🔒 Is my photo library private?

Absolutely. SwipeSort operates entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any server or shared with third parties. We respect your privacy and only request the necessary permissions to organize your photos.

↩️ Can I undo my actions?

Yes! After any action (organize, delete, or skip), an undo button appears. Tap it to reverse your last action. Additionally, deleted photos are moved to the Recently Deleted album where they can be recovered.

📁 How many albums can I create?

You can create unlimited custom albums to organize your photos any way you like.

🗑️ What happens to deleted photos?

When you delete a photo in SwipeSort, it moves to the "Recently Deleted" album. From there, you can permanently delete photos or restore them to your library. This gives you a safety net to recover accidentally deleted photos.

🔄 Can I move photos between albums?

Yes! Open any album, long-press on a photo to enter selection mode, select multiple photos, and use the "Move" option to transfer them to another album.

📸 Does SwipeSort modify my original photos?

No. SwipeSort only organizes photos into albums and manages deletions. Your original photos and their metadata remain unchanged.

🌙 Does SwipeSort have dark mode?

SwipeSort is designed with a beautiful dark interface that's easy on the eyes and perfect for extended photo sorting sessions.

⚡ How do I organize photos faster?

For maximum speed, use swipe gestures instead of tapping buttons. Swipe right to organize, left to delete, and up to skip. The card-based interface provides instant visual feedback as you work through your library.

🔍 Can I zoom in on photos?

Yes! Double-tap any photo to open the full-screen viewer where you can pinch to zoom, pan around, and examine photos in detail.

💾 Will my organized albums sync with iCloud?

Albums created in SwipeSort are stored in your local photo library. If you have iCloud Photos enabled in iOS Settings, your albums will sync across your devices through iCloud.

Troubleshooting

App is not showing my photos

Make sure you've granted SwipeSort full access to your photo library:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap SwipeSort
  3. Tap Photos
  4. Select "Full Access"

Photos are loading slowly

If you have iCloud Photos enabled and some photos are stored in the cloud, they may take a moment to download. Ensure you have a stable internet connection for the initial load.

Undo button not appearing

The undo button appears after you perform an action (organize, delete, or skip). It disappears after you perform another action or after a timeout.

Can't delete an album

The "Favorites" and "Recently Deleted" albums are system albums and cannot be deleted. All other custom albums can be deleted by tapping the trash icon next to them in the Albums tab.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is our priority. SwipeSort is built with privacy at its core:

  • All photo processing happens locally on your device
  • No photos are ever uploaded to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking of your photos
  • No account creation or personal information required
  • No third-party data sharing

SwipeSort only requests access to your photo library to provide its core functionality - organizing your photos. We never access, analyze, or transmit your photos outside of your device.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Create albums before sorting: Set up your albums in advance so you can quickly organize photos as you swipe
  • Use descriptive album names: Clear names help you find photos faster later
  • Sort in batches: Focus on one category at a time (e.g., vacation photos, screenshots)
  • Don't overthink it: SwipeSort is designed for quick decisions. If unsure, swipe up to skip
  • Use the full-screen view: Double-tap photos when you need a closer look
  • Review Recently Deleted: Periodically check this album before permanently deleting photos
  • Multi-select for bulk actions: Long-press in albums to select multiple photos for moving or deleting
